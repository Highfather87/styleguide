elements.sass

Elements are self-sufficient things, meaning:

  • they have a style of their own
  • they have no dependencies
  • they have no sub-classes
  • they can appear anywhere in the HTML

Think of it as atoms, the smallest entity possible.

The naming convention is singular words.
