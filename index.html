Logotype

The logotype is the most visible element of our identity — a universal signature across all TROUVA communications. Because the logotype is such a recognisable and highly visible brand asset, it is vital that it is always applied consistently wherever it appears.

When writing the TROUVA name in a sentence, always capitalize all letters.

Symbol

Our symbol comes in two versions, with and withouth a line. The symbol is based on the letter T from the main logotype.

It should be used for social media avatars, app icon and favicon.

Colours

Typography

The TROUVA typeface is Brown, a contemporary and versatile typeface that follows in the footsteps of modernism drawn and developed by Aurèle Sack.

Inspired by the grotesk typefaces of the early 20th century, specifically to the trailblazing design of Edward Johnston (1872-1944), who was commissioned to create a typeface for the Underground Electric Railways of London in 1913.

It is at the heart of the TROUVA identity and is the foundation for all TROUVA branding. Modern, clean, distinctive and legible.
